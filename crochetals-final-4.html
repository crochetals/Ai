FAQ

Got Questions? 💬

How long does delivery take?
+
Standard orders dispatch in 2-3 days. Custom orders take 5-7 days. Delivery across India takes 3-5 days after dispatch. COD available!
Can I customize colors and size?
+
Absolutely! DM us on Instagram @crochetals_ or WhatsApp us with your idea. We can customize color, size, flowers — everything!
Do you take bulk/wedding orders?
+
Yes! We love bulk orders for weddings, birthdays and events. Contact us 15+ days in advance. Special pricing available for bulk.
What if my product is damaged?
+
Share photos within 48 hours of delivery — we'll replace it or give a full refund. Your satisfaction is our priority! ♥
Is COD available?
+
Yes! COD available across most Indian pin codes. We also accept UPI, credit/debit cards and net banking.
